At Heriot-Watt University Malaysia, we believe in Positive Education - a commitment to nurturing both academic excellence and individual development. Our Undergraduate Programmes are career-focused to prepare students for real-world demands.

We offer a range of undergraduate programmes in the fields of Accounting, Actuarial and Data Science, Business, Built Environment, Computing Science, Engineering and Psychology.
