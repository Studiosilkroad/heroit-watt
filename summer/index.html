Are you looking for an exciting and enriching opportunity to expand your knowledge while immersing yourself in a vibrant culture?
Join our 2-week study programme in Malaysia for an unforgettable adventure where you'll have the chance to participate in a wide range of activities such as cultural workshops, language classes, and discovery tours. These activities will not only deepen your understanding and appreciation of Malaysian customs and traditions, but also provide you with valuable skills and experiences that you can apply to your studies and future career.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Programme 1: Understanding Malaysia
Our programme is specifically designed for international students who are interested in learning the Malay language and understanding Malaysian culture! Throughout the week, you will be introduced to the fundamentals of Malay vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, writing skills & cultural studies.
Our interactive activities and exercises provide ample opportunities for you to practice what you have learned. You'll be equipped with knowledge in Malay language and culture that establishes a solid foundation, enabling you to confidently engage with Malaysian cultures and communities.
 
 
Programme 2: Discovering Malaysia
Our programme offers a unique and exceptional chance for students to immerse themselves in the vibrant and diverse culture of Malaysia while expanding their knowledge.
Through a variety of activities such as cultural workshops, history lessons, as well as visits to significant cultural and historical sites, you can gain a profound understanding and appreciation of Malaysian customs, traditions, and way of life.
