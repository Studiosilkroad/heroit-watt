From flexibility to why one should pursue an MBA, join Associate Professor Jay Wasim, Head of Business and Management School, Heriot-Watt University Malaysia for our MBA Preview this 26 November 2022.

Together with his esteem colleagues, they will also share insights on HWUMANising Business with MBA, that are key to you and your organisation. Get an insight into studying MBA at Heriot-Watt University Malaysia by joining our MBA Preview class for free!

With a Masters in Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship and a PhD in Strategy, Enterprise and Innovation, Dr Wasim is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts and a Fellow of the Advance HE ( formerly Higher Education Academy) who teaches and supervises students at all levels of higher education from bachelors to PhD. He also brings his experience of consulting on growth and diversification of products and markets with businesses of all sizes, operating in several different sectors.
