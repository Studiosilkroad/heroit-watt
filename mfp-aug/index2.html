Making the right decision to continue your education journey is never easy. What programme am I interested in? Which university should I join? What will my university life be like? At the end of the day, what career progression do I want? I happen to have all these thoughts if not more, but I had my reasons why I chose Heriot-Watt University Malaysia (HWUM)>.

Being part of an education institution that offers world-class programmes, with a truly international reach of campuses in Malaysia, Dubai and in the UK, offering students the opportunity of a truly global learning experience was some of the reasons why I decided to be part of HWUM's community.

The University's commitment in ensuring we are 'Future Made', offering high-quality British education and the international study opportunities is evident with the Go Global inter-campus transfer programme and with the global network of students, scholars and alumni across the globe.

The support system by the academics and professional services, the friendly and caring community, the global opportunities and more, are what we really would need when we begin our university life.

I am proud of the 'Positive Education' approach that HWUM focuses on as it is beyond academic excellence, it helps to better understand yourself, discover a sense of purpose and mobilise that purpose into impact in our lives. We crafted our impact statements for this, and my impact statement is - I am an inspiration. My purpose is to inspire others to love and care for themselves while searching for their meaning in life and to then, carry on inspiring others.

These are just part of my reasons why my life changed being with HWUM, and you will learn more once you are a part of our community starting with the Welcome Week, where you will be introduced to the Fit-for-Future programme from 29 August - 2 September 2022. The programme will focus on developing your emotional intelligence, creativity, understanding your own personalities, your strengths, and weaknesses, and developing a long-term vision and mission. A very enjoyable programme!

On behalf of the student community here at HWUM, we are excited to welcome you soon and let us continue to build flourishing communities amongst us and as a globally connected university.
