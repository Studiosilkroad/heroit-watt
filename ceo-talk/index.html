Join us for theMBA Faculty CEO Lecture Series, a distinctive event presented by Edinburgh Business School at Heriot-Watt University Malaysia, tailored exclusively for MBA students.

Don't miss the opportunity to gain insights from our newly appointed Faculty CEO and distinguished speaker - Dr. Joshua Netto, who will be enlightening us on the topic 'Project Management - How Capital Projects is a Catalyst to Building a Better Nation'.

Dr. Joshua Netto is the President of PMI Malaysia and completed his masters in 2015 with a specialisation in strategic and project management. His day job is Director of Portfolio Management for Turner & Townsend Malaysia, a global consultancy for capital projects and programmes, and Joshua provides high-value advisory services for major international and local clients with a total of 23 years of experience.
