Calling All Teachers!
Join us for our 2023 Teachers Conference entitled, PURPOSE DRIVEN EDUCATION: UNLEASH THE SUPER-TEACHER IN YOU.

We are collaborating with Teach for Malaysia and Arus Academy to help you reconnect with your purpose in teaching. We will unlock your higher potential so that you can nurture the next generation of leaders. Everything will come to light in our specially curated Impact Statement workshop.
